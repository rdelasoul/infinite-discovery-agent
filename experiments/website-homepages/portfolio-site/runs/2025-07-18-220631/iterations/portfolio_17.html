About My Work

I'm a software engineer with a passion for developer tools and open source. Currently, I work at a fintech startup where I lead the developer experience team, focusing on internal tooling and workflow automation.

My approach to software is simple: build tools that solve real problems, share what you learn, and contribute back to the community. I believe the best software comes from developers who actively use what they build.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually experimenting with new technologies, contributing to open source projects, or writing about software development. I'm particularly interested in Rust, WebAssembly, and anything that makes developers more productive.
