## Typography

### **Font Personality**
Bold, contemporary, geometric, confident with technical precision. Innovative, readable, energetic, startup-appropriate professionalism.

### **Font Selection**
- **Headings**: Bold geometric sans-serif with strong character (Inter Black, Poppins Bold, Montserrat)
- **Body Text**: Modern sans-serif optimized for screens (Inter, SF Pro, Roboto)
- **Code/Technical**: Sophisticated monospace for developer credibility (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)

### **Typography Hierarchy**
- **H1**: 3rem (48px), font-weight 800, tight line-height (1.1) for impact
- **H2**: 2.5rem (40px), font-weight 700, confident line-height (1.2)
- **H3**: 2rem (32px), font-weight 600, clear hierarchy
- **Body**: 1.125rem (18px), font-weight 400, comfortable line-height (1.6)
- **Small**: 1rem (16px), slightly condensed for technical details

### **Text Treatment**
- **Letter Spacing**: Tight (-0.025em) for headings to create impact, normal for body
- **Line Height**: Balanced for energy and readability (1.5-1.6)
- **Alignment**: Left-aligned for clarity, center for impact moments
- **Effects**: Gradient text for headlines, subtle glows for CTAs, bold highlights

## Visual Style

### **Layout Philosophy**
- **Grid**: Systematic but bold - breaking grid for impact moments
- **Information Density**: High-energy with strategic white space for focus
- **Visual Weight**: Bold focal points with energetic asymmetrical balance

### **Spacing System**
- **Whitespace**: Strategic and purposeful - creates energy through contrast
- **Base Unit**: 4px for tight, energetic spacing (4, 8, 16, 24, 32, 48px)
- **Breathing Room**: Compressed for energy, expanded for important content

### **Visual Hierarchy**
- **Size Contrast**: Bold scale jumps (2x+ differences) for dramatic hierarchy
- **Color Usage**: Vibrant accents for CTAs, gradients for energy, high contrast for clarity
- **Positioning**: Dynamic layouts with diagonal elements and energetic compositions

### **Imagery & Media**
- **Photography**: High-energy, contemporary, tech-focused, diverse teams, modern workspaces
- **Image Treatment**: Sharp edges or subtle rounded corners (4px), dynamic overlays, gradient masks
- **Icons**: Bold, geometric, contemporary with consistent 2px stroke weight
- **Graphics**: Geometric patterns, gradient meshes, abstract tech-inspired illustrations

## Interaction & Animation

### **Interaction Personality**
- **Movement**: Snappy, energetic, confident - quick but polished
- **Timing**: Fast and responsive (150-250ms) to match startup energy
- **Feedback**: Bold and clear - users know immediately when something happens

### **Micro-Interactions**
- **Hover States**: Bold scale (1.05) with gradient shift, energetic glow effects
- **Button Interactions**: Strong press feedback (scale 0.95) with gradient animation
- **Form Feedback**: Dynamic border gradients, bold success/error states with animation

### **Transitions & Animations**
- **Page Changes**: Energetic slide transitions (200ms), zoom effects for impact
- **Content Loading**: Dynamic progress bars with gradient animation, skeleton screens with pulse
- **Navigation**: Fast slide-in menus, bouncy micro-animations for personality

### **Animation Timing**
- **Duration**: Quick (100-200ms) for feedback, snappy (200-300ms) for transitions
- **Easing**: Energetic ease-out, bounce effects for personality moments
- **Personality**: Confident and energetic, following startup pace and energy
